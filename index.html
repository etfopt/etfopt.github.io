How It Works
Four steps to your
ideal ETF portfolio

ETFopt transforms raw market data into a multi-dimensional scoring system, then personalizes recommendations around your exact preferences.

STEP 01
DEA Efficiency Filter
Risk and cost are treated as inputs; return, liquidity, and diversification as outputs. ETFs below an efficiency score of 0.8 are eliminated, leaving only high-quality candidates.
Target Reduction
STEP 02
AHP Preference Capture
Rather than asking you to fill in imprecise percentages, AHP pairwise comparisons mathematically quantify your preferences across 6 dimensions with a consistency check (CR value).
Preference Capture
STEP 03
Multi-Objective Optimization
A custom utility function replaces MPT's expected return, factoring in covariance across assets to produce a truly diversified allocation — not just a pile of similar ETFs.
Portfolio Optimization
STEP 04
Personalized Recommendations
Your ranked ETF list and allocation plan is generated, with FinBERT-powered sentiment signals alerting you when market conditions shift against your portfolio assumptions.
